Mediterranean Special: Chickpea Salad

Description

Ingredients

For the Dijon Dressing

  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 lemon juiced
  • 1 garlic clove minced
  • teaspoon Aleppo pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sumac
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1/4 cup Extra virgin olive oil

For the Salad

  • 2 cans chickpea
  • 1 large cucumber
  • 2 roasted red peppers
  • 1 small red onion
  • 1 cup chopped parsley
  • 2 cups tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1 avacado roughly chopped

Steps

  1. Make the dressing. In a large bowl, whisk together the Dijon, lemon juice, garlic, Aleppo pepper, sumac, and salt and pepper (about Â½ teaspoon each). Whisk, and while whisking, drizzle in enough olive oil to make it nicely glossy and well-balanced. Continue to whisk until emulsified.
  2. Mix. To the bowl with the dressing, add the chickpeas, cucumbers, tomatoes, roasted bell peppers, onion, parsley, and mint. Toss gently. Add the avocado and toss very gently once more.
  3. Enojoy! Taste and adjust the seasoning to your liking and serve immediately.
