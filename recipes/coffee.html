Iced Ocean Latte

Details

Ingredients

  • 20ml plant based milk
  • 60ml coconut water
  • 1/3 tsp Butterfly Pea Powder
  • Ice cubes to fill the glass
  • Whipped cream
  • 1 shot of Espresso

Steps

  1. Firstly, whip the plant-based cream and decorate the glass. Create the clouds on the sides of glass, using the whipped cream. Put ut the glass in the fridge.
  2. Mix the coconut water with Butterfly Pea Powder.
  3. Fill decorated glass with ice cubes.
  4. Pour the shot of espresso followed by the milk
  5. Pour over the blue water
